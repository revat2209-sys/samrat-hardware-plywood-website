Our Story

Bengaluru's Trusted
Hardware Showroom

Samrat Hardware & Plywood has been the go-to destination for homeowners, interior designers, and contractors across Bengaluru. Located in the heart of Anjanapura, our showroom brings together a curated collection of premium hardware, plywood, and interior fitting solutions under one roof.

We carry only genuine products from trusted brands — Hafele, Hettich, Ebco, Greenply, CenturyPly, and more — ensuring every purchase reflects lasting quality. Our expert team offers personalised guidance to help you choose the right products for every space in your home.
